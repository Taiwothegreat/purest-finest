Who We Are

Overview

We are focused on foods and beverages creating premium, plant-based, and natural foods and drinks. Offerings include REDEFINING REFRESHMENT WITH INTEGRITY AND INNOVATION vitality, vitamins, sports, drinks, and well-being foods made with organic ingredients to promote health and well-being. The company is known for using high-quality premium ingredients, alongside other carefully selected components designed to revitalize both body and soul. We ensure our brands are sustainable and beneficial for overall vitality, appealing to health-conscious consumers.

Origin

Inspired by the holistic wellness traditions of the plant-based finest ingredients, PureFinest® Natural Vitamin, Sports, and Vitality Drinks were born from the vision of Beulah Group Inc. in early 2016. Driven by a passion for organic ingredients and cutting-edge nutritional science, BGI crafted a line of beverages that not only revitalize the body but also nourish the soul. Pre-launched in the heart of London, England, on March 15, 2023, PureFinest® redefined the functional drink market by introducing a range that enhances everyday health with superior, organically sourced ingredients. Committed to purity and performance, PureFinest® has ushered in a new era of wellness beverages, seamlessly blending ancient wisdom with modern vitality.

The PFI Way

Our Purpose

Our mission is to enhance lives by offering high-quality food and beverages that inspire joy, promote well-being, and deliver satisfaction to the body and soul. We strive to be a reliable provider of innovative, wholesome products, thoughtfully created with care and excellence.

Our Vision

A vision of a future where every bite and sip support sustainability, exceptional quality, and well-being. We aspire to create a world where our food and beverages not only satisfy but also foster healthier lifestyles and contribute to a more sustainable planet.

Our Approach

With a commitment to quality and a focus on customer satisfaction, we blend premium ingredients, innovative technology, and eco-friendly methods to create food and beverages that excel in flavor and leave a lasting positive impact. This holistic approach ensures that everything we create not only delights the palate but also aligns with our values of wellness, sustainability, and innovation.
